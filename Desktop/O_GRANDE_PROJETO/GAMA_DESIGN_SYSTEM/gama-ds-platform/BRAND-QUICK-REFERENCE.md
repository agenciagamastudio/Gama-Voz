## 🎯 Brand Selection Guide

**Use this to choose the right brand for your project:**

### Studio is Best When...
- ✅ Building a brand identity from scratch
- ✅ Need creative, strategic thinking
- ✅ Focus on brand storytelling & impact
- ✅ Audience: Companies building brands

### TV is Best When...
- ✅ Advertising via TV or displays
- ✅ Need broadcast-quality production
- ✅ Focus on reach & measurable impact
- ✅ Audience: Large corporations, advertisers

### Rádio is Best When...
- ✅ Advertising via audio/podcasts
- ✅ Need engaging, personality-driven content
- ✅ Focus on audience engagement
- ✅ Audience: Brands with audio needs

### Calendários is Best When...
- ✅ Productivity & scheduling solutions
- ✅ Need clean, organized interfaces
- ✅ Focus on efficiency & time management
- ✅ Audience: Professionals, businesses

## 💡 Quick Implementation Guide

### For Designers
1. Use brand color as primary accent
2. Follow voice & tone guidelines
3. Match photography/imagery style
4. Test accessibility (4.5:1 contrast minimum)

### For Developers
1. Import `useBrand()` hook
2. Access `colors.primary` for brand color
3. Use `useBrandSwitcher()` for brand selection
4. All components auto-update with brand change

### For Content Writers
1. Adopt brand voice from quick reference
2. Use tone guidelines by context
3. Follow writing style in BRAND-GUIDELINES.md
4. Test with brand manager approval
